TAILOR MADE DESIGNS & SOLUTIONS ACCORDING TO SPECIFIC CUSTOMER REQUESTS.

Our facilities are located in Bursa, Turkey on a 8400 m2 covered area amongst 24000 m2 open grounds, with 120 employees including a team of 15 design engineers.

We specialize in the design, engineering and manufacture of tailor made machinery & equipment for the sanitary ceramic industry. We set up complete sanitary ware plants as turnkey projects. We provide solutions for other fields in the ceramic industry, along with filtration technologies, material handling solutions and stone splitting equipment.

Focusing marketing activities on export markets, we have achieved an export rate approaching 85% of turnover, expanding our customer base to over 40 countries worldwide. In particular we have a significant market share in the Middle East, North Africa, East Europe and Asian regions. In providing sanitary ware technologies & solutions, we are proud to include leading international producers such as Villeroy & Boch, Duravit, Roca and Kohler as our partners and customers.

We are a global brand with cutting edge technologies, continuous investment in Research & Development and environment-friendly technology, all backed up with a dedication to use the best components for its machines.

We constantly work on providing customer satisfaction and we are proud of our high level of repeat business. The key to our success is that we merge quality with optimum levels of technology, resulting in feasible investment costs for equipment designs that satisfy our customer needs.
