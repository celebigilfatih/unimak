Preparation

We offer experience and expertise in manufacturing of tailor made equipment needed for each process of sanitary ware production, from raw material preparation to finished product handling and packaging.

  • HIGH PRESSURE CASTING PLANTS
  • TAILOR-MADE PLASTER MOULD CASTING PLANTS/SOLUTIONS

The core of the production process, the current state of the art of sanitary technology excellence. Thanks to the experience gained over 30-plus years, Sacmi can guarantee certain results and total control over the production process. High pressure casting with nearly a thousand installed machines is completed by traditional casting, resin moulds and modelling, drying, and robotised glazing thanks to 3 Gaiotto robot models - with well over a thousand units installed. Riedhammer, German health care technology and beyond, produces kilns ensuring excellent output performance.

The HWS is a modular structure kiln lined with cordierite or ceramic wool and can be used for first firing, second firing and decoration firing. The advanced combustion system and the dimensional flexibility of this kiln offer ideal solutions for specific customer needs.
